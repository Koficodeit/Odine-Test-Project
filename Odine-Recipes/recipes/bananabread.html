Banana Bread

Banana bread is a classic, comforting baked good that combines the sweet, rich flavor of ripe bananas with a moist, tender crumb. Often enjoyed as a breakfast treat or a dessert, this quick bread is easy to make and loved for its versatility. It can be customized with various add-ins such as nuts, chocolate chips, or dried fruits. The natural sweetness of overripe bananas not only enhances the flavor but also keeps the bread wonderfully moist. Perfectly sliceable and delicious whether served warm or at room temperature, banana bread is a timeless favorite for all ages.

Ingredients

  • Ripe Bananas
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the Pan: Grease a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  3. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth. Stir in the melted butter.
  4. Add Sugar and Egg: Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  5. Add Dry Ingredients: Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture, then mix in. Add the flour last, and stir until just combined.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool and Serve: Let the banana bread cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack before slicing.
