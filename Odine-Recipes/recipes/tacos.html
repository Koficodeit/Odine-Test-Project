Tacos

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish known for their vibrant flavors and versatility. They consist of a folded or rolled tortilla, traditionally made from corn or flour, filled with a variety of ingredients. Common fillings include seasoned meats such as beef, chicken, or pork, as well as beans, cheese, and a variety of fresh vegetables. Tacos are typically garnished with toppings like salsa, guacamole, cilantro, onions, and lime, offering a delightful mix of textures and tastes. Whether enjoyed as a quick street food snack or as part of a larger meal, tacos are celebrated for their ability to be customized to individual preferences and regional styles.

Ingredients

  • Con or Flour Tortillas
  • Ground Beef or Chicken
  • Onions
  • cilantro
  • Salsa or Pico De Gallo

Steps

  1. Cook Meat: Cook ground beef or chicken in a pan over medium heat until fully cooked. Add taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions. Simmer until thickened.
  2. Prepare Toppings: Chop onions, cilantro, and prepare salsa or pico de gallo.
  3. Warm Tortillas: Warm corn or flour tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble Tacos: Place the meat in the center of each tortilla. Add desired toppings such as onions, cilantro, salsa, and a squeeze of lime.
  5. Serve: Serve immediately, hot and fresh.
