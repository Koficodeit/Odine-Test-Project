Pasta

Pasta is a versatile and beloved staple in many cuisines, particularly Italian. Made from durum wheat semolina or flour mixed with water or eggs, pasta comes in a variety of shapes and sizes, each suited to different types of sauces and dishes. From long strands of spaghetti and flat ribbons of fettuccine to tubular penne and tiny orzo, pasta can be paired with rich, hearty sauces like marinara and Alfredo, or enjoyed in lighter preparations such as pesto and aglio e olio. Its adaptability and comforting taste make pasta a favorite for simple weeknight meals as well as elaborate gourmet creations.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  • Cook Pasta: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  • Prepare Sauce: In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant. Add tomatoes or tomato sauce and simmer.
  • Season: Season with salt, pepper, and herbs like basil or oregano. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes.
  • Combine: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  • Serve: Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
