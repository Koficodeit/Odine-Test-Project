Lasagna

Lasagna is a hearty and flavorful Italian dish that layers wide, flat pasta with a rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and a generous topping of melted mozzarella. Baked to golden perfection, this comfort food is known for its satisfying combination of textures and flavors, from the tender pasta to the savory filling and the gooey, cheesy top. Often seasoned with garlic, herbs, and spices, lasagna is a versatile and beloved meal that can be customized with various ingredients to suit different tastes. Perfect for family dinners or gatherings, it’s a crowd-pleaser that embodies the warmth and richness of home-cooked cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sausage
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Brown the ground beef or sausage and mix with marinara sauce.
  2. Prepare Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and some parsley.
  3. Assemble Lasagna: Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, a layer of meat sauce, and a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  5. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for about 10 minutes before serving.
